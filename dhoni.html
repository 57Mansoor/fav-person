what do you like from him

Fans love MS Dhoni for his calm demeanor, exceptional leadership, and ability to perform under pressure. Known for his cool, composed attitude on the field, he led the Indian cricket team to major victories, including the 2007 ICC T20 World Cup, 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and 2013 ICC Champions Trophy. His finishing skills, especially in tight situations, earned him the nickname *Captain Cool*. Fans also admire his humility, tactical brilliance, and the way he always puts the team first. Off the field, his down-to-earth personality and love for his family make him even more relatable and respected.
