Hrithik Roshan is a popular Indian actor known for his versatile roles in Bollywood films. Born on January 10, 1974, he made his debut in 2000 with the film *Kaho Naa... Pyaar Hai*, which was a massive success. Over the years, he has become one of the top actors in the industry, starring in films like *Jodhaa Akbar*, *Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara*, *Krrish*, *War*, and *Dhoom 2*. Hrithik is celebrated for his impressive acting skills, exceptional dancing talent, and dedication to fitness. He is also known for his down-to-earth personality and has earned a reputation as a humble and grounded individual despite his fame. Beyond acting, he is involved in charitable work, particularly in education and healthcare. Hrithik is also an advocate for mental health awareness, having spoken openly about his struggles with stammering. In addition to his professional success, Hrithik is admired for his family values and positive influence on social media.
